Harmonics Unleashed

How might we

Create an interactive museum exhibit for children to experience the joys of music creation.

About

In this project, I set out to help both children and adults explore the connection between sound and play. In Harmonics Unleashed, every button press or movement creates both a sound and a corresponding waveform on screen, turning music into something you can see and interact with.

Play

I started by taking apart several children's toys that I felt created fun, tactile experiences. I also experimented with forming buttons and knobs using parts sourced from a local hardware store.

Challenges

A key challenge was making sure both kids and adults could enjoy the exhibit. I wanted children to play as hard or as gently as they wanted without parents worrying about anything breaking, so I built the enclosure and buttons from solid wood, shaped like oversized building blocks.

Shapes

The shapes of the buttons and knobs relate to the sounds they control. For example, the spaceship distorts the audio, while the robot adds a glitch like effect.
